Digital Storeroom

A digital solution for managing a shop storeroom, allowing users to efficiently track, add, and search for products.

Background

Features

Displaying Storeroom Layout

Visualize the arrangement of products within the storeroom for easy navigation and management.

Connecting to an External Database

Connect to an external database to store and retrieve product data, ensuring data persistence and scalability.

Adding and Deleting Products

Add new products to the storeroom or remove outdated items as needed, keeping the inventory up to date.

Searching for Products

Quickly search for products using various filters and criteria, making it simple to find the information you need.
