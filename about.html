About Me

School

I attended school at St John's RC Academy in Perth. As this was an all through school, I have been there from nursery right through to S6, with my year group being the first to move through every year.

During this period, I managed to achieve 7 National 5s, 6 Highers and 3 Advanced Highers.

In S6 I was part of the Pupil Leadership Team, being deputy head boy. This involved helping with large school events, as well as pushing for changes within the school to help better it for pupils and staff.

University

University of Dundee

I am currently attending the University of Dundee, where I am in my first year of a Computing Science degree. In the future, this degree includes the oppertunity of a year in industry, which I am very much looking forward to.

So far, I have started to learn a new programming language, Java, as well as developing my skills in web development and python.

This year has already provided me with my first experience with a unix based system, which was a slight challenge at first but a welcome addition.

It is my hope that this degree will provide me with a strong foundation in computing science, as well as the skills and experience needed to pursue a career in the tech industry, whatever that may entail at the time of graduation.

Work

Currently, I work part time at Gloagburn Farm Shop, which is a local farm shop in Perth. I have been working there since mid 2024, helping to serve customers, stock shelves and keep the shop tidy.

I have really enjoyed working there, as it has given me a lot of experience in customer service and working in a team.

Extra Activities

Tennis

Perth Lawn Tennis Club

Outside of school, I haved played tennis for 14 years, at Perth Lawn Tennis Club. Over this time, I have competed in a number of Club Championships, as well as representing the club in leagues at both junior and senior level.

For around 3 years, I have helped out with coaching at the club, at both the weekly junior coaching sessions, as well as the major camps which run across the school holidays.

Clarinet

I have also played the clarinet for 8 years, from Primary 6 through to S6. Over this time I have performed many times individually at school showcases and events, as well as part of the school orchestra.

During this time, I have also been a member of Perth Wind Ensemble, then Perth Wind Orchestra, and finally Perth Youth Orchestra. This involved performing at a number of concerts across Perth, as well as a few events in Edinburgh as part of the Edinburgh Festival Fringe.
