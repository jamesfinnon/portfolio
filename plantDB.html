Plant Species Database

A searchable database application for cataloguing and managing plant species information, designed to help users efficiently store, retrieve, and update plant data.

Background

Features

Menu System

Navigate the application using a clear and intuitive menu system, allowing access to all major features.

Adding and Deleting Plants

Add new plant entries to the collection or remove existing ones as needed, keeping the database up to date.

Sorting the Collection

Sort the plant collection by various metrics, such as species name, water needs, or sunlight requirements.

Viewing the Whole Collection

Display all plants in the collection in a clear, organized format for easy browsing and management.

Calculating the Evaporation Rate

Calculate the evaporation rate for each plant or the entire collection, helping to optimize watering schedules and plant care.
